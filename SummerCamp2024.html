Welcome to Royal Kingdom Soccer Camp 2024

Meet Coach Roey Kivity, your soccer camp expert with over 20 years of experience in the beautiful game! Having played in some of the highest levels of soccer across America, Coach Kivity has a wealth of knowledge and skills to share. He has been part of professional teams such as Maccabi Petah Tikvah, Bik'at Hayarden, and Bney Eilat F.C. Not only did he play professionally, but he also trained under the guidance of soccer legends at prestigious academy teams like Real So Cal Academy.


His incredible journey in soccer includes learning from former pro players such as Jermaine Jones, Dema Kovalenko, Hanan Azulay and Beto Dos Santos. These top-tier coaches passed on their deep understanding and passion for the game, enriching Coach Kivity's own coaching style.


At our soccer camp, Coach Kivity brings this rich history and firsthand experience to help young athletes thrive both on and off the field. Whether your child is just starting out or looking to elevate their game, Coach Kivity is excited to guide them to new heights! Join us for a camp experience filled with fun, learning, and soccer excellence.
