About Royal Kings Football Academy

Founded in 2018, Royal Football Academy was designed to grow yound and talented players to play at the highest level. Our mission is to develop skilled, confident, and team-oriented players through a focus on technical excellence, physical fitnesss, and psychological growth.

Our experianced coaches bring a wealth of knowledge and personal commitment to each players growth, both on and off the field. Join us to be a part of a thriving community that values hard work, respect, and sportsmanship.

Roey Kivity played at the highest levels of soccer in America and Israel. He began his career in Los Angeles by playing for FCLA where his team won the Dallas Cup against all academies in United States. He continued his career by joining one of the most prestigious teams in America. Real So Cal Academy and played all 4 years against the best competition throughout America. After COVID he was scouted to play in University of California Merced. During his first semester he got invited to play on the #1 Ranked Academy team in Israel called Maccabi Petah Tikvah and helped his team get to the State Cup Final. He then joined his firsts Adult team Bik'at Hayarden at the age of 19. During the Winter season he decided to move to Bnei Eilat F.C. In 2022 he moved to Santa Monica College. In 2023 Kivity recorded 17 goals and 12 assists in a 20 game season. Kivity was nominated MVP by his teammates and was accepted into the first team all stars by the Western Conference coaches.

Roey Kivity has been coached by professionals all around the world such as Jermain Jones, Hanan Azulay, Dema Kovelanko, Beto Dos Santos, Joey Hoffman, Sasha Khapsalis, Dror Zilpa, Terry Davila, Milan Radovic, Jose Luis, Zack Fieldman, Tim Pierce, Rick Minars, and many more.
